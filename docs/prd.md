# Gym Demo Website Requirements Document

## 1. Website Overview

### 1.1 Website Name
FitZone Gym

### 1.2 Website Description
A professional gym website designed to showcase facilities, services, and attract potential members. This demo website serves as a comprehensive presentation tool for gym owners to understand the digital presence possibilities for their fitness business.

## 2. Core Features

### 2.1 Homepage
- Hero section with compelling gym imagery and call-to-action buttons
- Brief introduction to the gym and its unique value proposition
- Quick overview of key services and facilities
- Membership plans preview section
- Testimonials from satisfied members
- Contact information and location map
- Live class counter showing current ongoing classes
- Featured promotions banner

### 2.2 About Us
- Gym history and mission statement
- Introduction to trainers and staff with photos
- Facility highlights and equipment showcase
- Certifications and achievements
- Virtual tour of the gym facilities
- Core values and philosophy

### 2.3 Services & Programs
- Detailed list of fitness programs offered (e.g., personal training, group classes, yoga, CrossFit, cardio training)
- Class schedule and timetable
- Program descriptions with benefits
- Specialized programs (weight loss, muscle building, rehabilitation)
- Nutrition counseling services
- Online training options

### 2.4 Membership Plans
- Different membership tiers with pricing
- Plan comparison table showing features of each tier
- Special offers and promotions
- Membership benefits overview
- Free trial pass option
- Corporate membership packages
- Student and senior discounts

### 2.5 Class Schedule
- Weekly class timetable
- Filter options by class type, instructor, or time
- Class descriptions and difficulty levels
- Real-time class availability status
- Upcoming special events and workshops

### 2.6 Gallery
- Photo gallery showcasing gym facilities, equipment, and training sessions
- Before and after transformation photos (with permission)
- Video gallery of workout sessions and testimonials
- Event photos from gym competitions and community activities

### 2.7 Trainers
- Individual trainer profiles with photos
- Qualifications and specializations
- Brief bio for each trainer
- Trainer availability calendar
- Client success stories per trainer

### 2.8 Contact Page
- Contact form for inquiries
- Phone number and email address
- Physical address with embedded map
- Operating hours
- Social media links
- FAQ section
- Live chat support option

### 2.9 Blog/News Section
- Fitness tips and articles
- Gym updates and announcements
- Success stories
- Workout routines and exercise guides
- Nutrition advice and healthy recipes
- Industry news and trends

### 2.10 Member Portal
- Member dashboard for tracking workouts and progress
- Class booking system
- Personal training session scheduling
- Membership renewal and payment management
- Access to exclusive member content and resources

### 2.11 Testimonials & Reviews
- Dedicated page for member testimonials
- Star ratings and written reviews
- Video testimonials
- Success story highlights

### 2.12 Facilities & Equipment
- Detailed equipment list with specifications
- Facility amenities (locker rooms, showers, parking)
- Safety and hygiene protocols
- Accessibility features

### 2.13 Events & Challenges
- Upcoming fitness challenges and competitions
- Community events calendar
- Registration for special events
- Past event highlights and results

## 3. Technical Notes

- Website should be responsive and mobile-friendly
- Fast loading times for optimal user experience
- Clear navigation structure for easy browsing
